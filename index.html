Who I Am

My name is Faith Flynn, I am currently a junior at UW-Madison and a direct admit to the BBA program at the university.
I am a marketing major pursuing a certificate in digital studies. I plan on graduating in December of 2021. Although I am
just beginning my journey in the business world, I believe my eagerness to improve my skills in the field and my readiness

manager of a music or fashion corporation and push for women in the business world to dream big. I want to create a career
for myself that I enjoy everyday, can express myself creatively in, and am passionate about.
